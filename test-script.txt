FADE IN:

INT. COFFEE SHOP - DAY

A bustling coffee shop in the heart of downtown. SARAH (28), ambitious screenwriter, sits alone at a corner table, laptop open, fingers hovering over keyboard.

SARAH
(to herself)
Come on, Sarah. Just one good scene. That's all you need.

She takes a deep breath, closes her eyes, and begins typing furiously.

INT. MOVIE SET - DAY

A chaotic film set. DIRECTOR (50s, stressed) shouts through a megaphone.

DIRECTOR
Action! Cut! What was that? You call that acting?

ACTOR (20s)
I thought it was good emotional range.

DIRECTOR
Emotional range? You look like you just smelled bad milk! Again!

The actor sighs, frustrated. This is clearly not going well.

EXT. PARK - SUNSET

Sarah walks through the park, script in hand, deep in thought. She stops at a bench.

SARAH
(whispering)
Maybe I'm not cut out for this. Maybe I should just... give up.

A MYSTERIOUS FIGURE appears from the shadows.

MYSTERIOUS FIGURE
Never give up on your dreams. Especially when they're this close to coming true.

Sarah looks up, shocked.

SARAH
Who... who are you?

MYSTERIOUS FIGURE
(smiling)
I'm opportunity. And I'm knocking.

The figure extends a hand. Sarah hesitates, then takes it.

FADE OUT.

THE END
